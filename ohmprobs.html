Eight (simple) problems (like ones you'll see on tests):

  1. Name what each of these symbols stand for: Ω, V, A

  2. What is the voltage of a circuit with a current of 7 amps and a resistance of 3 Ohms?

  3. What is the current of a circuit with a voltage of 10 volts and a resistance of 4 Ohms?

  4. What is the resistance of a circuit with a voltage of 13 volts and a current of 4 amps?

  5. If resistance is doubled, what happens to the current and the voltage?

  6. If voltage is doubled in a circuit and resistance is halved, what will happen to the current?

  7. If current is halved in a circuit, what will happen to the voltage and the resistance?

  8. Explain the concepts of voltage, current, and resistance in your own words.
    (if you can explain a concept precisely, then, chances are, you understand it pretty well.)
